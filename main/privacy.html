Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2025

Your privacy matters to us at StudyZen. Here's how we handle your data responsibly and securely:

  1. What We Collect
    • Account Information: First name, optional last name, username, email, and password (for regular accounts).
    • Classroom Info: Class name, optional section, and teacher name (for class accounts).
    • User Content: Timetables, notes, and tasks you save.
    • Access Logs: Basic usage data (non-personal), such as login times.
  2. How We Use Your Data
    • To save and restore your study-related content.
    • To personalize features for class and individual accounts.
    • To improve the functionality and reliability of the platform.
  3. Data Sharing
    • We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.
    • Admins may access classroom data for authorized educational purposes only.
  4. Data Security
    • All data is stored securely using Firebase infrastructure.
    • We take steps to prevent unauthorized access or data loss.
  5. Your Rights
    • You may request to view, correct, or delete your data.
    • Contact us if you have privacy concerns or want data removed.
